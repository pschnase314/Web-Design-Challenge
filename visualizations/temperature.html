Max Temperature vs. Latitude

This graph shows max temperature as a function of latitude. The temperature appears to depend fairly strongly on latitude. The highest temperatures are found a bit north of the equator, with temperatures falling in both directions. This is as expected, given that the measurements were taken during northern hemisphere summer, when the most direct sunlight is found a bit north of the equator.
